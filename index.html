Follow the Money — Analysis

QUANTUM • MAY 2026

The $2B Quantum Equity Play: What It Means for Public Markets

The Department of Commerce’s decision to take equity stakes in nine quantum companies (including IBM and GlobalFoundries) marks a clear shift from pure grants to public-private ownership models.

Stock Implications: Pure-play quantum names (IONQ, RGTI, QBTS) saw sharp moves on the news. The deal validates the entire sector and reduces near-term funding risk for participants. Long-term winners will likely be companies with strong hardware roadmaps and the ability to scale manufacturing domestically.

Key tickers to watch: IONQ, RGTI, QBTS, IBM

CRITICAL MINERALS • 2025-2026

DoD’s Equity Strategy in Rare Earths: A New Playbook

By taking direct equity positions (MP Materials) and offering price floors + revenue sharing, the Department of Defense is moving beyond traditional grants and loans. This creates stronger alignment and gives the government upside in strategic supply chains.

Stock Implications: This model is likely to expand. Companies with domestic assets and the ability to produce finished products (magnets, processed materials) are best positioned. Watch for more deals involving offtake agreements and government co-investment.

Key tickers to watch: MP, LAC
